Biote

As CEO, I led Biote through its transformation from a promising growth-stage company to a publicly traded leader in hormone optimization therapy. This journey required building every capability a public company needs—governance, compliance, investor relations, and commercial infrastructure.

The Challenge

Biote had proven its clinical model and established product-market fit, but lacked the infrastructure, governance, and commercial capabilities needed for public markets.

The Outcome

Successful NASDAQ IPO, expanded commercial reach, and built the leadership team and operational systems to sustain growth as a public company.
