About Terry

A career defined by building healthcare companies that make a difference.

Background

Throughout my career, I've been drawn to healthcare companies at critical inflection points—moments when the right leadership can mean the difference between promising potential and lasting impact.

My path has taken me from [early career] through increasingly complex leadership roles, culminating in my tenure as CEO of Biote, where I led the company through its NASDAQ IPO. Along the way, I've learned that building great healthcare companies requires more than operational excellence—it demands a genuine commitment to the patients and providers we serve.

Leadership Philosophy

I believe the best healthcare companies are built on three foundations: clinical integrity, operational discipline, and an unwavering focus on patient outcomes. When these elements align, everything else follows—sustainable growth, engaged teams, and the trust of investors and partners.

In the boardroom, I bring this same perspective. I ask the hard questions about clinical value and patient impact. I push for the operational rigor that turns strategy into results. And I advocate for the long-term thinking that builds companies capable of lasting success.
