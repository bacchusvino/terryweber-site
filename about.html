About Terry

A career defined by transforming companies at critical inflection points.

Background

Throughout my career, I've been drawn to companies at critical inflection points—moments when the right leadership can mean the difference between promising potential and lasting impact.

My path has taken me from retail and automotive through healthcare leadership, culminating in my tenure as CEO of Biote, where I led the company through its NASDAQ IPO in 2022. Along the way, I've learned that building great companies requires more than operational excellence—it demands a genuine commitment to the customers and communities we serve.

Leadership Philosophy

I believe the best companies are built on three foundations: operational discipline, financial rigor, and an unwavering focus on outcomes. When these elements align, everything else follows—sustainable growth, engaged teams, and the trust of investors and partners.

In the boardroom, I bring this same perspective. I ask the hard questions about value creation and risk management. I push for the operational rigor that turns strategy into results. And I advocate for the long-term thinking that builds companies capable of lasting success.
