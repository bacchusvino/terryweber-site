Board Value

What I bring to the boardroom

My career has been defined by taking companies through critical inflection points—whether that's preparing for an IPO, navigating regulatory complexity, or building the commercial infrastructure to scale. Boards hire me when things are on fire and they need a grownup.

SPAC Survivor

Took Biote public via SPAC in 2022 and stayed as CEO for years afterward. Most SPAC CEOs fail or get replaced. I landed it, executed it, and sustained performance.

Corporate Firefighter

I fix broken operations in operationally brutal industries—auto parts, lingerie, brain-health clinics, hormone therapy. I modernize brands, rebuild leadership, and scale profitably.

Founder Whisperer

Founder-led companies mean chaos, emotion, and lack of process. I fix the mess, keep the founder happy, AND scale the company. Very few CEOs can do all three.

Six-Industry Pattern Matcher

Healthcare, retail, automotive, neuroscience, beauty, finance. Most CEOs master one vertical. I read patterns across all of them. That's rare at the national level.

SEC Audit Committee Qualified

Boards desperately need directors who understand audit and risk. This credential makes me immediately recruitable for public company boards.

Large-Scale Operator

500+ employee companies, multi-location clinic networks, national retail chains, complex supply chains. Most CEOs never handle that scale. I've done it repeatedly.
